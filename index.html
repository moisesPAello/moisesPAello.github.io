Things I've shipped

In daily production use

Sella Cruce

Bank-statement reconciliation, automated.

The problem

The team reconciled bank statements by hand — cross-checking every movement in a bank's PDF statement against an Excel of expected transactions, line by line. Slow, and easy to miss a match.

What I built

A web app that ingests the bank PDF and the expected-transactions Excel, cross-references the movements, and marks the matches automatically. I designed it around the exact manual process the team already used, so it fit their workflow on day one.

Outcome

In daily production use at JGRP by real users, running containerized on an internal server. I own it end to end — design, development, deployment, and ongoing maintenance.
